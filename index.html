Before you ask.

Is this safe? Are you reading my emails?

No. We set up the connection — we don't sit in the middle of it. Permissions are scoped and revocable from your own Google/Notion account settings at any time. We never store your data on our side.

Do I need a paid Claude plan?

You need a Claude account with MCP/connector support, which is available on free and paid tiers depending on Anthropic's current plans. We'll confirm exactly what you need during intake before any work starts.

What if it breaks after setup?

You get 14 days of free fixes. After that, small fixes are a flat $40; if you want ongoing support, we offer an optional $19/month maintenance plan — no pressure either way.

Can you connect tools other than Gmail and Notion?

Yes — HubSpot, Shopify, Slack, Calendly, and most tools with public APIs. Mention what you use during intake and we'll confirm feasibility before you pay anything.

I'm not techy at all. Will I understand any of this?

That's who this is for. You never touch a terminal or a config file. The walkthrough video is built for someone seeing this for the first time.

How do I pay?

We accept USDT (crypto via any wallet) and direct bank transfer. After you reach out we'll confirm the details before any work starts. No payment is taken until you've had a chance to ask questions and confirm you're happy to proceed.
